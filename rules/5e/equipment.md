## Weapon Properties

| Property | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Ammunition** | Requires a specific type of ammunition. Recovering used ammunition: half can be recovered after a battle with 1 minute of searching. |
| **Finesse** | Use either STR or DEX modifier for attack and damage rolls (same modifier for both). |
| **Heavy** | Small and Tiny creatures have disadvantage on attack rolls with this weapon. |
| **Light** | Can be used for two-weapon fighting. |
| **Loading** | Requires an action or bonus action to reload; only one attack per action/bonus action regardless of Extra Attack. |
| **Range** | Listed as (normal/long) in feet. Attacking beyond normal range has disadvantage. Cannot attack beyond long range. |
| **Reach** | Increases the weapon's reach by 5 ft for melee attacks and opportunity attacks. |
| **Thrown** | Can be thrown to make a ranged attack within the listed range. Use STR modifier for damage (or DEX if finesse). |
| **Two-Handed** | Requires two hands to attack. |
| **Versatile** | Can be used with one or two hands. Listed as *versatile (die)* — use the listed die when wielded in two hands. |
| **Special** | See the weapon's individual entry for unusual rules. |

## Encumbrance

**Standard rule:**
- Carrying capacity = STR score × 15 lb
- A character can push, drag, or lift up to STR score × 30 lb (speed drops to 5 ft while doing so)

**Variant encumbrance rule (optional):**
- Encumbered (STR × 5 lb): Speed −10 ft
- Heavily encumbered (STR × 10 lb): Speed −20 ft, disadvantage on ability checks, attack rolls, and saving throws that use STR, DEX, or CON

---

## Silvered Weapons

Some creatures (e.g., lycanthropes, certain devils) are immune or resistant to non-magical damage but not to silvered weapons. Silvering a weapon or 10 pieces of ammunition costs **100 gp**.

---

## Improvised Weapons

A character can use any object as a weapon. The DM determines appropriate damage (usually 1d4) and whether the throw range applies (typically 20/60). If the object resembles a weapon, it deals the weapon's listed damage.
