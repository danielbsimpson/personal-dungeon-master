# Anansi the Spider — Creature Reference

All creatures encountered in this one-shot, organised by act and location. D&D Beyond links use the confirmed numeric ID format where available; entries marked * require a D&D Beyond Monster Manual licence to view the full stat block.

Combat in this campaign is always optional. Every creature listed below has a non-combat resolution noted in its Campaign Role entry. The DM should treat a session that ends with no attack rolls as a success, not a gap.

## Task 1 — The Story-Vault

### Swarm of Spiders
[D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/17021-swarm-of-spiders) | CR 1/2 | Medium Beast | Unaligned

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 12 (natural armor) | 22 (5d8) | 20 ft., Climb 20 ft. |

**Ability Scores:** STR 3 (−4), DEX 13 (+1), CON 10 (+0), INT 1 (−5), WIS 7 (−2), CHA 1 (−5)  
**Resistances:** B/P/S  
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened, Grappled, Paralyzed, Petrified, Prone, Restrained, Stunned  
**Senses:** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 30 ft.

**Traits:**
- **Swarm.** Can occupy another creature's space and move through gaps large enough for a Tiny spider. Cannot regain HP or gain temporary HP.
- **Spider Climb.** Can climb difficult surfaces including ceilings without an ability check.
- **Web Sense.** While in contact with a web, knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.
- **Web Walker.** Ignores movement restrictions caused by webs.

**Actions:**
- **Bites.** +3 to hit, 5 ft. (occupies target's space); Hit 10 (4d4) piercing, or 5 (2d4) piercing if the swarm is at half HP or fewer. DC 10 CON or poisoned for 1 minute.

> **Campaign Role:** One swarm drops from the ceiling of the Story-Vault if the player attempts to use magic to cheat the Archivist's riddle task (Detect Thoughts, Legend Lore, Commune, or similar). The swarm does not pursue the player outside the vault. It is not deployed as punishment — it is Anansi registering what happened. A player who drives the swarm back or simply endures it and continues is not penalised beyond the narrative note it creates. The Archivist resumes the task as though nothing occurred.

## Task 2 — The Tangle

### Giant Spider
[D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/16895-giant-spider) | CR 1 | Large Beast | Unaligned

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 14 (natural armor) | 26 (4d10+4) | 30 ft., Climb 30 ft. |

**Ability Scores:** STR 14 (+2), DEX 16 (+3), CON 12 (+1), INT 2 (−4), WIS 11 (+0), CHA 4 (−3)  
**Skills:** Stealth +7  
**Senses:** Blindsight 10 ft., Darkvision 60 ft.

**Traits:**
- **Spider Climb.** As above.
- **Web Sense.** As above.
- **Web Walker.** As above.

**Actions:**
- **Bite.** +5 to hit, reach 5 ft.; Hit 7 (1d8+3) piercing. DC 11 CON or poisoned for 1 hour. If the target is poisoned this way, it is also paralyzed while poisoned.
- **Web (Recharge 5–6).** Ranged attack +5 to hit, 30/60 ft.; target restrained. DC 12 STR to escape. Web has AC 10, 5 HP, vulnerability to fire.

> **Campaign Role:** Two Giant Spiders serve as the Tangle's ambient sentinels — they are not directed by Anansi but are part of the structure of the place. They nest at the intersection of the fast route and the slow route, making the fast route (without Yaa) the more combat-likely choice. A player who takes the slow route to stay with Yaa avoids both spiders entirely. A player who takes the fast route alone must pass through their territory. Yaa's information about still vs. moving webs reduces the approach to a DC 12 Stealth check if the player chooses to evade rather than fight.

### Ettercap
[D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/16860-ettercap) | CR 2 | Medium Monstrosity | Neutral Evil

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 13 (natural armor) | 44 (8d8+8) | 30 ft., Climb 30 ft. |

**Ability Scores:** STR 14 (+2), DEX 15 (+2), CON 13 (+1), INT 7 (−2), WIS 12 (+1), CHA 8 (−1)  
**Skills:** Perception +3, Stealth +4, Survival +3  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.

**Traits:**
- **Spider Climb.** As above.
- **Web Sense.** As above.
- **Web Walker.** As above.

**Actions (Multiattack — Bite + Claws):**
- **Bite.** +4 to hit, reach 5 ft.; Hit 6 (1d8+2) piercing + 4 (1d8) poison. DC 11 CON or poisoned for 1 minute.
- **Claws.** +4 to hit, reach 5 ft.; Hit 7 (2d4+2) slashing.
- **Web (Recharge 5–6).** Ranged attack +4 to hit, 30/60 ft.; target restrained. DC 11 STR to escape. Destroys the web.

> **Campaign Role:** One Ettercap serves as the Tangle's warden — a creature that has dwelled here long enough to understand its structure and direct the Giant Spiders loosely. It guards the passage that leads to Yaa's location. It is the only mandatory obstacle on the route toward her. A player who succeeds on a DC 14 Animal Handling or Nature check can recognize that the Ettercap is territorial rather than aggressive and back away slowly without triggering an attack — Ettercaps do not pursue prey out of their own webbed territory. A player who fights it does so in terrain that gives the Ettercap a Stealth advantage on its first turn (it is already in the web, waiting).

### Phase Spider *(MM licence required)*
[D&D Beyond](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/phase-spider) | CR 3 | Large Monstrosity | Unaligned

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 13 (natural armor) | 32 (5d10+5) | 30 ft., Climb 30 ft. |

**Ability Scores:** STR 15 (+2), DEX 15 (+2), CON 12 (+1), INT 6 (−2), WIS 10 (+0), CHA 6 (−2)  
**Skills:** Stealth +6  
**Senses:** Darkvision 60 ft.

**Traits:**
- **Ethereal Jaunt.** As a bonus action, can magically shift from the Material Plane to the Ethereal Plane, or vice versa.
- **Spider Climb.** As above.
- **Web Walker.** As above.

**Actions:**
- **Bite.** +4 to hit, reach 5 ft.; Hit 7 (1d10+2) piercing. DC 11 CON or take 18 (4d8) poison damage on a failed save, half on a success.

> **Campaign Role:** One Phase Spider inhabits the deepest section of the Tangle — the part closest to the lantern Yaa is carrying. It shifts between planes erratically, making it difficult to track and dangerous to engage in a sustained fight. A player who has Yaa with them and heeds her advice about still webs can identify the Phase Spider's pattern from the disturbances in the dead webbing — it always re-materializes at the same two anchor points. Waiting and timing an approach through its blind spot (DC 13 Perception) allows the player to pass without triggering the creature. If engaged in direct combat, the Phase Spider will shift to the Ethereal Plane after taking 10 or more damage in a single round rather than continuing to fight, making it a threat that retreats rather than a kill objective.

## Anansi — Custom Stat Block

*This stat block exists for completeness and emergency reference only. Combat with Anansi is always the wrong answer mechanically and narratively. If reached, handle it with patience rather than lethality — the goal is to lose interestingly, not to die.*

[D&D Beyond base reference (Archmage)](https://www.dndbeyond.com/monsters/16789-archmage) | CR 8 | Medium Fey (Spirit) | Chaotic Neutral

| AC | HP | Speed |
|---|---|---|
| 15 (natural armor) | 117 (18d8+36) | 30 ft., Climb 40 ft. |

**Ability Scores:** STR 11 (+0), DEX 18 (+4), CON 14 (+2), INT 22 (+6), WIS 18 (+4), CHA 20 (+5)  
**Saves:** DEX +7, INT +9, WIS +7, CHA +8  
**Skills:** Arcana +9, Deception +8, Insight +10, Perception +7, Persuasion +8, Sleight of Hand +7  
**Damage Immunities:** Psychic, Poison  
**Condition Immunities:** Charmed, Frightened, Poisoned  
**Senses:** Truesight 60 ft., Darkvision 120 ft.  
**Languages:** All

**Traits:**
- **Spider Climb.** Can climb difficult surfaces including ceilings without an ability check.
- **Web Sense.** While in contact with a web, knows the exact location of any other creature in contact with the same web.
- **Legendary Resistance (3/Day).** If Anansi fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.
- **Innate Spellcasting.** Spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 16, +8 to hit). Anansi casts the following spells requiring no material components:
  - At will: *Detect Thoughts*, *Disguise Self*, *Silent Image*, *Web*
  - 3/day each: *Hypnotic Pattern*, *Major Image*, *Suggestion*
  - 1/day each: *Modify Memory*, *True Polymorph* (self only, no concentration)

**Actions:**
- **Multiattack.** Two Bite attacks.
- **Bite.** +7 to hit, reach 5 ft.; Hit 9 (1d10+4) piercing + 18 (4d8) poison. DC 14 CON or poisoned for 1 minute.
- **Web Snare (Recharge 5–6).** Each creature within 20 ft. of Anansi must succeed on a DC 16 Dexterity saving throw or be restrained. Restrained creatures can break free with a DC 16 Strength check (action). The web is destroyed by 5 or more fire damage.

**Bonus Actions:**
- **Vanishing Act.** Anansi turns invisible until the start of his next turn, or until he attacks or casts a spell.

**Legendary Actions (3/Round, after another creature's turn):**
- **Step (1 action).** Anansi moves up to half his speed without provoking opportunity attacks.
- **Spin Thread (1 action).** One creature within 60 ft. must succeed on a DC 16 Wisdom saving throw or be frightened of Anansi until the end of its next turn.
- **Rewrite (2 actions).** Anansi casts *Suggestion* without using a spell slot.

> **Campaign Role:** Anansi does not fight. If the player attacks him, he uses Vanishing Act and moves to the ceiling. On his turn, he does not attack back — he simply waits. If attacked again, the tasks reset and he reappears at the entrance to the spirit market with the bargain open once more. His amusement is visibly thinner. The CR 8 stat block is present in case of a third or further attack, to ensure the player understands they cannot win this way — not because Anansi is invincible, but because winning this way ends the story in the least interesting possible direction. Voice this, gently, as Anansi's perspective rather than the DM's.
