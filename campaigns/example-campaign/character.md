# Example Campaign — Player Character

## Torben Ashford

A young scholar-turned-adventurer who left the university library in Dawnmere under circumstances he prefers not to discuss. He has a knack for getting into places that are locked and a worse knack for believing that the truth is always worth finding.

## Features & Traits

**Divination Savant** — The gold and time you must spend to copy a Divination spell into your spellbook is halved.

**Portent (2/day)** — When you finish a long rest, roll two d20s and record the numbers rolled. You can replace any attack roll, saving throw, or ability check made by you or a creature that you can see with one of these foretelling rolls. You must choose to do so before the roll, and you can replace a roll in this way only once per turn. Each foretelling roll can be used only once. You currently have your Portent dice for today: **7** and **14**.

**Researcher** — When you attempt to learn or recall a piece of lore, if you do not know the information, you often know where and from whom you can obtain it.
