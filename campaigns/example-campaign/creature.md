## Vault Ooze (Flooded Vault)

A grey-brown gelatinous mass the size of a large dog, formed from decades of decomposing parchment, ink, and residual magic. It is not aggressive — it feeds on organic material it finds, not on things that are actively moving away from it.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Ooze |
| **Size** | Small |
| **AC** | 8 |
| **Hit Points** | 22 (4d8 + 4) |
| **Speed** | 10 ft, swim 10 ft |
| **Challenge** | 1/4 (50 XP) |

### Stats

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 12 (+1) | 6 (−2) | 13 (+1) | 1 (−5) | 6 (−2) | 1 (−5) |

### Traits
- **Amorphous** — The ooze can move through a space as narrow as 1 inch wide without squeezing.
- **Ink Sense** — The ooze can detect written text and parchment within 30 ft as if with blindsight.

### Actions
- **Pseudopod.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) bludgeoning damage. The target's non-magical paper items (books, scrolls, maps) must succeed on a DC 11 Dexterity saving throw or take 1d4 acid damage (item is damaged/destroyed on a failure).

### Encounter Notes
The ooze guards the lower archival chamber through no intention of its own. It simply congregates near the largest remaining collection of parchment — which is Mira's central reading table. The player can lure it away by creating a trail of parchment or organic material, or bypass it entirely using the upper shelf walkway. It ignores creatures who do not touch or destroy what it is feeding on.

## Coldstone's Warden (Inner Vault)

A **Helmed Horror** variant. Mira Coldstone constructed this guardian herself and bound it with specific, narrow instructions. It is not malevolent. It is precise.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Construct |
| **Size** | Medium |
| **AC** | 20 (plate armor, shield) |
| **Hit Points** | 60 (8d8 + 24) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft |
| **Challenge** | 4 (1,100 XP) |

### Stats

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 18 (+4) | 13 (+1) | 16 (+3) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) | 10 (+0) |

### Saving Throws
- STR +6, CON +5

### Damage Immunities
- Lightning, Poison

### Condition Immunities
- Blinded, Charmed, Deafened, Frightened, Paralyzed, Petrified, Poisoned

### Senses
- Truesight 60 ft, darkvision 60 ft, passive Perception 10

### Traits
- **Magic Resistance** — The Warden has advantage on saving throws against spells and other magical effects.
- **Spell Immunity** — The Warden is immune to *Sleep*, *Charm Person*, and *Hold Person*.
- **Bound Purpose** — The Warden was instructed to prevent removal of three specific sealed containers (the red-wax canisters). It does not attack creatures who do not attempt to take these items or open the sealed door to the inner sanctum.

### Actions
- **Multiattack.** The Warden makes two Longsword attacks.
- **Longsword.** *Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft, one target. *Hit:* 8 (1d8 + 4) slashing damage, or 9 (1d10 + 4) if used two-handed.

### Non-Combat Resolution
The Warden will stop attacking if the player abandons the sealed containers and withdraws to the archival chamber. It cannot speak, but it gestures: it points at the three red-wax canisters, then at the door, then raises one gauntlet flat — *stop*. A DC 12 Insight check lets the player understand its meaning. If the player puts down whatever they were carrying and steps back, the Warden steps aside. It will not allow the canisters to leave the vault. It is not protecting the vault from the player — it is protecting something inside the canisters *from the world outside*.

## Millhaven Watch Guard (Town, non-hostile)

Ordinary town militia. Present if the player causes trouble in town. Not a combat encounter under normal circumstances.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| **Type** | Humanoid (human) |
| **AC** | 13 (chain shirt) |
| **Hit Points** | 11 (2d8 + 2) |
| **Speed** | 30 ft |
| **Challenge** | 1/8 (25 XP) |

### Actions
- **Spear.** *Melee or Ranged Weapon Attack:* +3 to hit, reach 5 ft or range 20/60 ft, one target. *Hit:* 4 (1d6 + 1) piercing damage, or 5 (1d8 + 1) if used two-handed.
