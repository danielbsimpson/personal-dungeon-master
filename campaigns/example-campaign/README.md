# The Sunken Vault of Mira Coldstone

A D&D 5e introductory one-shot designed for a single player of level 1–3, set in a rain-soaked riverside town and the flooded vault beneath it.

## Overview

The player character arrives in **Millhaven** — a quiet trading town on the banks of the River Sorn — chasing a rumour about a drowned wizard's vault flooded by the river decades ago. The townsfolk want it left alone. A local merchant wants it opened. The truth about why Mira Coldstone flooded her own vault is more unsettling than either party suspects.

**Tone:** Low-stakes mystery with creeping dread, grounded in small-town politics
**Setting:** Millhaven and the Sunken Vault beneath the old mill
**Themes:** Greed vs. legacy, what scholars leave behind, what the river keeps

## One-Shot Design Notes

| Element | Details |
|---|---|
| **Recommended Player Level** | 1–3 (single player) |
| **Estimated Play Time** | 2–3 hours |
| **Combat Role** | Optional — the vault can be fully explored without combat if the player is careful and clever. The guardian encounter has a non-combat resolution. |
| **Key DM Principle** | Mira Coldstone is not a villain. She made a choice. The player can agree or disagree with it, but the vault is not a dungeon to be looted — it is an archive to be understood. |
